# CodeClean Bot — Refund Policy

## Digital Credits
All purchases are for digital processing credits used to clean code files via Telegram.

## Refund Eligibility
You may request a refund within 48 hours of purchase if:
- The service failed to deliver a cleaned file after payment
- Credits were not added to your account after payment

## How to Request
Email: Miserbot.ai@gmail.com

Include:
- Your Telegram username
- Date of purchase
- Amount paid

## Processing Time
Refunds are processed within 5 business days.

## Non-Refundable
Credits that have already been used for cleaning are non-refundable.
